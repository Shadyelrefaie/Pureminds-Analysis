07 — Recommendations

What Should Change

🌐 Website Improvements
1
Fix the SEO Foundation — Add Server-Side Rendering
The website needs to either switch to a framework like Next.js (SSR/SSG) or add proper meta tags and structured content that search engines can read. Without this, no content strategy investment will generate inbound leads.
🔴 Critical Priority
2
Build Dedicated Service Pages with Keyword Intent
Create individual pages for each service: "Event Management Riyadh," "TV Commercial Production Saudi Arabia," "Corporate Identity Design KSA." Each page should target specific search queries their clients type into Google.
🔴 Critical Priority
3
Add Case Studies with Measurable Results
Every major project (Mudad campaign, Saudi Film Forum) should have a results page: brief, the concept, the execution, and the outcome. Even qualitative outcomes count. This is the #1 trust signal for B2B buyers.
🔴 Critical Priority
4
Redesign the User Journey with a Clear CTA Architecture
The site needs three clear conversion paths: (1) "Request a Proposal" for clients, (2) "View Our Work" for consideration, (3) "Join Our Team" for recruitment. Each path should have its own flow and form.
🟡 High Priority
💼 LinkedIn Strategy Improvements
5
Rewrite the LinkedIn About Section — Front-Load the Proof
The current About section leads with a positioning statement but buries the proof. Rewrite it so the first two lines (visible before "See more") contain a specific credential: years in market, a notable client category, or a quantified result. B2B buyers decide in the first 3 seconds whether to keep reading.
🔴 Critical Priority
6
Shift from "Project Announcements" to "Value-First Content"
Move beyond "we did this project." Start publishing content that answers what their clients are thinking: "How to brief a production company," "What makes a national event successful," "The 3 mistakes brands make before a product launch." This builds authority between projects.
🔴 Critical Priority
7
Add Algorithm-Native Formats: Carousels, Polls, Documents
LinkedIn rewards native content. A carousel titled "Inside our production of [X]" or a poll "What's the #1 challenge for event planners in KSA?" generates 3–5x more reach than a photo post with a caption.
🟡 High Priority
8
Build an Editorial Calendar: 3 Posts Per Week, Consistent Pillars
Recommended pillars: (1) Behind the Work — BTS stories from projects, (2) Industry Insights — event trends, production tips, Vision 2030 commentary, (3) Team & Culture — humanizing the brand for recruitment and trust. Bilingual: Arabic + English on rotation.
🟡 High Priority
9
Activate Employee Advocacy — LinkedIn's Most Underused Growth Lever
With 184 employees, Pureminds has a massive untapped amplification network. Encouraging 10–15 key employees (Creative Director, Project Managers, Account Leads) to post consistently from their personal profiles and tag the company page can 10x organic reach.
🟢 Medium Priority
